About Tremor

What is tremor?

Tremor is a bodily phenomenon whereby shaking is experienced that is outside the individual's control. This occurs in everyone, but is not clinically significant in most individuals. The result is a set of rhythmic, involuntary muscle contractions that decreases stability and steadiness of affected body parts.
